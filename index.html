About Me

I am currently a second year Computer Science student studying at the University of British Columbia.

I enjoy learning about all the different "layers" of software technology. I have interest in the server, network, and hosting environment, as well as data modelling and API usage. I am also passionate about user experience, to enhance customer loyalty through improving usability and ease of use between applications and their audiences. Prototyping and wireframing are some of my areas of interests. Finally, I am keen on great user interface design. My background in fine arts and graphic design have given me great appreciation for aesthetically pleasing applications and software.

I learn best through hands-on activities, such as hackathons or projects, and I love working with others to discover new ideas!
